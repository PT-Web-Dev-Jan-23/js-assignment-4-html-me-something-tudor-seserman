Bagels, am I right?

Where to start with these self contained nuggets of joy? These circular marvels are simultaneously dense, light, chewy, and soft. They can stand alone, anchor a morning meal, be dressed up with lox , be dressed down with a smear of cream cheese. Naturally, though controversially, as pizza bagels are the perfect anytime snack.
